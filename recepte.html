Solanka

  • Time: 80 min
  • Serves: for 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
beef 600g
boiled ham 100g
sausages 80g
kidneys 120g
onions 200g
pickles 200g
tomato puree 80g
butter 40g
capers 80g
olives 60g
sour cream 100g
lemon 1
spices on your taste

Step 1: Boil the broth from the beef and bones. Boil for at least 45 minutes.

Step 2: Cut the onions into straws and fry in butter, add tomato puree and saut√© soft.

Step 3:Cut pickles into thin slices. In the boiling broth put cucumbers, boil, add pre - fried onions, sliced, cooked meat products (beef, ham, boiled kidneys, sausages), olives, capers, pepper, bay leaves, salt and cook for 15 minutes.

Step 4: Before serving solanka put sour cream, parsley greens, lemon slices. Lemon slices, if desired, can be arranged in a dish and served on the table separately, without placing solanka.

Roasted pork with vegetables and mushrooms

  • Time: 120 min
  • Serves: for 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
pork 1 kg
potatoes 500g
mushrooms 200g
carrots 300g
onion 1
spices on your taste

Step 1: Wipe the piece of meat, rubbed with salt and put the skin down in a baking pan.

Step 2: Cut onions in half and carrots cut into large slices, pour a little water under the meat and put in a hot oven.

Step 3:When the meat is browned, pour it over hot water or broth and continue frying on low heat, frequently watering. Cut the half - baked piece upside down, cut the skin into small squares with a sharp knife and bake until the roast is ready.
At this point, add the peeled, sliced potatoes and mushrooms to the pan.

Step 4: To make the skin softer, it is smeared a few times with butter.
Finished roast a little cool, then cut into neat slices and arrange in a baking dish.

Step 5: Pour hot water over the pan, bring to a boil, allow the excess fat to settle, add the flour mixed with cold water, heat, strain and serve with roasted vegetables.

Source: Roasted pork with vegetables and mushrooms

Sweet rice cream

  • Time: 90 min
  • Serves: for 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
rice 200g
milk 1 l
sugar 6 tbsp
vanilla sugar 25 g
sweet cream 500g
salt pinch
gelatin - optional 25 g

Step 1: Rinse the rice and bring the milk to a boil, adding 2 tbsp sugar, vanilla and salt.

Step 2: Cook a little, take off the heat, leave to plump. After an hour, you can start checking that all the milk has been absorbed into the rice.

Step 3:When the rice is dry, add the swollen and melted gelatin (the amount depends on how tight you want it may not be at all) and allow to cool.

Step 4: When the rice is almost cold, whipped cream with the remaining sugar and mix with rice. Whipped cream can be put more or less, a matter of taste. Serve with cranberry sauce.

Source: Sweet rice cream
